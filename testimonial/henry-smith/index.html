About GCR

GCR Infrastructures, has been the principal contractor and has equally remained as one of the elite infrastructure. We have been instrumental in executing the mass earthwork, control blasting, back filing, demolition work and infrastructure development alongside the roads, drains, utility pipeline etc. We have also been able to get our hands-on over some of the prestigious projects from across Telangana.

As a part of our contracting services, we have been able to extend our hands with reference to the deployment of some of the heavy Earth Movers, Rock Breakers, Compactors, Rollers and other machineries for the timely completion of the project which is assigned to us. During the process of commissioning, we are absolutely committed to meet the expectation of our customers through a well-defined and designed service delivery system.
